About Us

North Augusta MTB Club is a fun-loving, laid back group of individuals who enjoy spending time outdoors and all terrain biking. Over the past year, we've grown from a few friends telling anyone who will listen about the sport to a group of about twenty-five still pretty guilty of that second part. If you come spend some time out on the local trails, with or without us, we're confident you'll see why we're so excited about the sport. After all, getting outside and having some good, not so clean fun is what it's all about!

Weekly Rides

We go on rides as a group once a week, usually on Saturday in the morning or early afternoon. Any upcoming ride listed as "Easy" is family-friendly and kids are welcome. Upcoming rides listed at higher difficulties may not be suitable for younger kids unless they're very experienced. A minimum of one ride a month will be family-friendly and one ride every other month will be significantly more challenging at "Advanced" or higher difficulty. Everyone is, of course, welcome to come, but please bear in mind that rides which exceed your current skill level may prove to not be very much fun.
